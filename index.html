HEALTHABLES

Healthables is a nutritionist approved disposable lunchbox that contains a delicious and healthy balanced meal. This lunchbox comes in different flavorful varieties that are suitable for all ages, all kinds of taste palates and diets.

Healthables had undergone through revisions and strict reviewing by professionals to guarantee that it meets the needed nutritional value for your body.

The meal includes a main dish, a drink and desert that ensures to be filling, tasty and satisfying. The contents of Healthables will boost your health, especially, when it is combined with your exercise routine.
